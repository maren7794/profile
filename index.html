My Favorite Cities

San Francisco

I spent my last summer in a town near the city. I especially liked how close it is to the ocean and the mindset of the people.

Marrakech

I visited that city a couple of years ago and fell in love with the culture and the people. It is a huge city but people still use donkeys and horses for transportation. The streetmarkets are very authentic and you can always find what you are looking for.

Istanbul

I spent a few days here with my family. A friend of ours who grew up there showed us the city and the best places to go. The turkish culture is very courteous, especially when you have Turkish friends. The Grand Bazar is very impressive and offers everything you could wish for, for little money.
